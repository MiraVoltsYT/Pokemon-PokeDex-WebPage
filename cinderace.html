Biological Overview

Cinderace possesses incredible core strength and balance. By juggling pebbles with its feet, it transforms them into flaming soccer balls. The heat from these projectiles is enough to leave third-degree burns even with a glancing blow.

Behavioral Patterns

Highly competitive and prone to showing off, Cinderace thrives under the pressure of a crowd. It is known to get "in the zone" during battle, where its reflexes become so fast it can dodge attacks before they are even launched.

OFFENSIVE STRATEGY

Pyro Ball (Signature Move)

The user ignites a small stone and kicks it at the target. This move has a massive **120 Power** and a 10% chance to burn. It is considered one of the most efficient physical Fire moves in existence.

Libero (Hidden Ability)

Cinderace’s ultimate tactical advantage. It changes its type to the move it is about to use. This provides an offensive boost to every single attack in its moveset, making it impossible to predict defensively.

Tournament Gear
  • Heavy-Duty Boots: Negates damage from entry hazards like Stealth Rock.
  • Life Orb: Boosts damage at the cost of slight HP drain.
