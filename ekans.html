#0023 - SNAKE POKÉMON

EKANS

Poison

RECON PARAMETERS

HP 35
ATTACK 60
DEFENSE 44
SP. ATTACK 40
SP. DEFENSE 54
SPEED 55

ARCHIVE: THE SILENT CREEPER

Vibrational Detection

Ekans curls up in a spiral while at rest. This position allows it to quickly respond to threats from any direction. It flickers its tongue to "taste" the air and detect the presence of prey.

Swallow Reflex

It can detach its jaws to swallow large prey whole. However, after a meal, it becomes heavy and sluggish, requiring time to digest the food in a safe hiding spot.

Egg Predation

It is a notorious egg thief, often slipping into the nests of Pidgey or Spearow when the parents are away. It is mostly silent when moving through tall grass.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Intimidate / Shed Skin (Abilities)

Intimidate lowers the opponent's Attack stat upon entering battle. Shed Skin gives it a chance to heal status conditions like paralysis or sleep naturally.

Signature Moveset
  • Wrap: Constricts the target for several turns, preventing escape and dealing steady damage.
  • Poison Sting: A quick strike that has a high chance of leaving the target poisoned.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Black Sludge: Gradually restores HP for Poison-types, but damages any other type that tries to hold it.
  • Eviolite: Boosts defenses significantly since Ekans is not fully evolved.
