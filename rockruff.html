Biological Overview

Rockruff is famous for its persistence; once it starts a hunt, it won't stop until it catches its target. It communicates affection by rubbing the sharp stones on its neck against people—a greeting that is notoriously painful for trainers but proves its loyalty. Its sense of smell is sharp enough to track a target for miles.

TRAINING & STRATEGY

The "Own Tempo" Speciality

Only Rockruff born with the **Own Tempo** ability possess the mental fortitude required to reach the rare **Dusk Form Lycanroc**. This variant cannot be confused in battle, making it a reliable physical attacker when faced with complex status conditions.

Aggression Warning

When Rockruff's eyes begin to glow red at sundown, it is a biological indicator of peak energy. During this phase, it becomes increasingly wild and difficult to control, signaling that evolution into its more dangerous adult form is imminent.

Hazard Setter

In competitive play, Rockruff excels as a utility lead. By using **Stealth Rock**, it creates a persistent threat on the opponent's side of the field, while moves like **Rock Tomb** help control the opponent's Speed.
