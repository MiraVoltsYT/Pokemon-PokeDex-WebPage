Core Specifications
Species Log
Rockruff is a loyal Pokémon that will never forget a kindness. However, as it nears evolution, it becomes more aggressive and will howl at the moon.
Biometric Observations
  • Collar: Composed of sharp rocks that it rubs against others to show affection.
  • Olfactory: An incredible sense of smell used to track targets across canyons.
  • Temperament: Excellent for beginners, though difficult to manage during its "wild phase."
Evolutionary Trigger
Evolution path is determined strictly by temporal conditions (Time of Day) and internal aptitude (Own Tempo ability).
