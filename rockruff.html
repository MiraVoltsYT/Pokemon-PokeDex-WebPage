Biological Overview

Rockruff has a habit of pressing the rocks in its neck against those it's fond of. While a sign of affection, these rocks are incredibly sharp, making this a painful greeting for trainers. As it grows, it becomes more aggressive and starts to howl at the sun.

TRAINING NOTES

Stealth Rock (Specialty)

Rockruff is excellent at setting traps. It can scatter pointed stones around the enemy field, damaging any Pokémon that switches into battle.

Own Tempo (Ability)

This Pokémon is so focused on its own rhythm that it cannot be confused by opponents. Only Rockruff with this specific ability can evolve into the rare **Dusk Form Lycanroc**.

Behavioral Warning

When Rockruff is close to evolving, its temperament becomes wild. If it begins to disappear at night or act more temperamental, it is a sign that its evolution is imminent.
