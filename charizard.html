Biological Overview

Charizard possesses massive, powerful wings that allow it to reach altitudes of over 4,600 feet. Its fire breath is said to reach temperatures high enough to melt solid stone and glaciers. Despite its intimidating power, a well-trained Charizard will never vent its full heat on an opponent it deems weaker than itself.

The Pride of the Flame

It is a competitive creature by nature. Experienced Charizard are often seen flying across the horizon in search of powerful opponents to test their strength. The more battles it survives, the hotter its tail flame burns, turning from orange to a brilliant, searing white-hot intensity.

Territorial Dominance

Charizard is an apex predator of the sky. It is highly protective of its nesting grounds and will scorch any intruder that it feels poses a threat to its young. It is the gold standard for many Kanto region trainers.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Solar Power (Hidden Ability)

Under a bright sun, Charizard’s Special Attack is boosted by a massive 50%. However, this comes at the cost of its physical vitality, draining its HP slightly every turn. This makes it a devastating "Sun-Sweeper" in competitive play.

Elite Moveset
  • Blast Burn: The ultimate Fire-type move. It incinerates everything in its path but leaves Charizard exhausted for the next turn.
  • Roost: Charizard lands and recovers 50% of its max HP, shedding its Flying-type weaknesses for a brief moment.
Legendary Gear
  • Heavy-Duty Boots: Critical for survival. It prevents the 50% HP damage Charizard usually takes from switching into Stealth Rocks.
  • Charizardite Y: If available, this stone triggers Mega Evolution, summoning instant Sunlight to boost its fire power.
