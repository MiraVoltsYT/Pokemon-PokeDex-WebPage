Species Log
Skeledirge's gentle singing soothes the souls of all who hear it. It burns its enemies to a crisp with more than 5,400-degree-Fahrenheit flames.
Spectral Anatomy
  • Fire Bird: The flame on its snout has taken the form of a bird, powered by its soul.
  • Gorgon Breath: Flames emitted from its mouth can reach temperatures that incinerate steel instantly.
  • Ghostly Resonance: Its songs bypass physical barriers to strike the target directly.
Tactical Note
Torch Song is its signature move. Not only does it inflict massive damage, but it also boosts Skeledirge's Special Attack every time it is used.
