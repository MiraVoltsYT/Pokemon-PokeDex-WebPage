BIOLOGICAL DATA

The Eternal Rivalry

Zangoose has been in a blood feud with Seviper for generations. The memory of this rivalry is literally etched into its DNA; the moment it smells a Seviper, the fur on its back bristles and it enters a state of uncontrollable aggression.

Combat Stance

While it usually walks on all fours, it stands on its hind legs when it becomes enraged. Its claws are its greatest pride, kept sharp enough to rend thick steel plates with a single "Crush Claw" strike.

⚠️ RIVAL DETECTED: SEVIPER

Zangoose is immune to Seviper's venom thanks to its **Toxic Boost** ability, which actually makes it stronger when poisoned.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Toxic Boost (Hidden Ability)

When Zangoose is inflicted with Poison, its Physical Attack power increases by 50%. This makes it a terrifying "Glass Cannon" on the battlefield.

Signature Moves
  • Crush Claw: A brutal slash that has a high chance of crushing the opponent's defensive posture.
  • Close Combat: Zangoose abandons all defense to unleash a flurry of strikes, ending the fight quickly.
Tactical Gear

Trainers often give Zangoose a **Toxic Orb**. It poisons the Zangoose immediately, triggering its Toxic Boost for maximum damage from turn one.
