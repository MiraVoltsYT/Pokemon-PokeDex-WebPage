ARCHIVE: THE SHORT-TEMPERED RAIDER

Incessant Shrieking

It flaps its short wings to flush out insects from the tall grass. It then plucks them with its short, sturdy beak. It is known for its loud, high-pitched cry that can be heard from half a mile away.

Aerial Aggression

Unlike Pidgey, Spearow has a very violent disposition. It is highly territorial and will attack anything—human or Pokémon—that enters its space. It uses its sharp beak to deliver rapid-fire pecks.

Low-Altitude Flight

Because its wings are short, Spearow cannot fly at high altitudes or for long distances. Instead, it excels at low-altitude, high-speed maneuvers used to ambush prey.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Keen Eye / Sniper (Abilities)

Keen Eye ensures it never misses due to accuracy drops. Sniper increases the damage of critical hits, making its aggressive playstyle even more lethal.

Signature Moveset
  • Peck: A basic but fast jabbing attack using its sharp beak.
  • Fury Attack: A rapid-fire succession of 2 to 5 stabs in a single turn.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Sharp Beak: Maximizes the damage output of its Peck and Drill Peck attacks.
  • Focus Sash: Necessary due to its extremely low defensive stats; allows it to land at least one strike.
