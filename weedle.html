BIOLOGICAL DATA: THE TOXIC CRAWLER

The Cephalic Stinger

The 2-inch barb on its head is sharp and filled with a potent toxin. Weedle uses this stinger to ward off attackers, specifically bird Pokémon that try to pluck it from the leaves. The bright color of its body serves as a warning to predators: "Do not touch."

Olfactory Detection

Weedle has an extremely acute sense of smell. It uses its large, round red nose to distinguish between its favorite types of leaves and those that are bitter or rotten. It can detect a predator’s scent from several yards away.

Daily Diet

It eats its own weight in leaves every day. This high calorie intake is the only way it can sustain the energy required for its lightning-fast evolution into Kakuna.

TACTICAL ANALYSIS

Shield Dust (Ability)

Like its counterpart Caterpie, Weedle possesses Shield Dust. This prevents enemies from flinching it or landing secondary status effects, ensuring Weedle can at least attempt to poison the target before fleeing.

Signature Moveset
  • Poison Sting: A physical jab with its head stinger. It has a high 30% chance of leaving the target poisoned.
  • Bug Bite: Allows Weedle to consume the opponent's held berry, gaining its effects and denying the enemy a heal.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Focus Sash: Vital for surviving a hit from a faster opponent to guarantee a Poison Sting lands.
  • Poison Barb: A small, sharp claw that increases the power of Weedle's Poison-type moves by 20%.
