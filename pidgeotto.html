ARCHIVE: THE TERRITORIAL HUNTER

Vast Territory

Pidgeotto claims a huge area as its own territory. It flies in circles, keeping a sharp eye out for prey or intruders. If its territory is violated, it shows no mercy in clawing the intruder.

Visionary Predator

Its visual acuity is phenomenal. No matter how high it flies, it can clearly distinguish the movements of its prey on the ground, such as Exeggcute or Magikarp.

Endurance Flight

Unlike its previous form, Pidgeotto possesses the stamina to fly for days at a time. It uses its powerful talons to carry prey back to its nest, often over hundreds of miles.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Keen Eye / Big Pecks (Abilities)

Big Pecks prevents its Defense stat from being lowered by the opponent, ensuring it maintains its durability during long aerial dogfights.

Signature Moveset
  • Wing Attack: Strikes the target with large, spread wings. A reliable source of physical damage.
  • Roost: Lands on the ground to rest, recovering up to half of its max HP.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Eviolite: Dramatically increases its survivability, making it a surprisingly tanky mid-stage Pokémon.
  • Flyinium Z: (Specialized) Allows for the use of devastating aerial Z-Moves.
