Biological Log
It flies at speeds equal to a jet fighter plane. It never allows its prey to escape. Garchomp are known to live in volcanic caves and high-altitude mountain ranges, preying on bird Pokémon.
Physical Anatomy
  • Scales: Microscopic ridges that reduce drag in flight and during sand-burrowing.
  • Fins: Sharp enough to slice through thick trees when flying at high speeds.
  • Sensory: Protrusions on the head act as radar to track distant movement.
Behavorial Pattern
When it folds up its body and extends its wings, it looks like a sonic plane. It is highly aggressive during sandstorms, using the dust for concealment.
