ARCHIVE: THE HYDRO-CANNON TITAN

Pressurized Artillery

The rocket cannons on its shell fire jets of water capable of punching holes through thick steel. Their aim is remarkably precise; they can hit a discarded tin can from a distance of over 160 feet. The recoil from firing is so immense that Blastoise must plant its heavy feet firmly into the ground to avoid being knocked back.

Structural Integrity

Blastoise’s shell is incredibly thick and heavy, designed to withstand high-pressure impacts. It intentionally makes itself heavy to withstand the back-pressure of its own water jets. It is often used by firefighters and rescue teams for its ability to clear debris with water pressure.

Oceanic Sentinel

In the wild, Blastoise are the undisputed leaders of Squirtle and Wartortle colonies. They are naturally protective and will use their massive bodies to shield smaller Pokémon from predators like Sharpedo.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Mega Launcher (Mega Ability)

When Mega Evolved, Blastoise develops a third, massive cannon. This ability increases the power of "pulse" and "aura" moves (like Water Pulse or Dark Pulse) by 50%, turning it into a long-range tactical sniper.

Elite Moveset
  • Hydro Pump: A high-volume blast of water that deals massive damage but has slightly lower accuracy.
  • Shell Smash: A risky tactic where Blastoise breaks its own shell to drastically boost its Speed and Offensive stats at the cost of its Defense.
Legendary Gear
  • White Herb: Used alongside Shell Smash to immediately restore its lowered defensive stats.
  • Blastoisinite: The core required for Mega Evolution and the activation of Mega Launcher.
