BIOLOGICAL DATA: THE THERMAL CHRYSALIS

Thermal Reorganization

While almost incapable of movement, the internal temperature of a Kakuna is incredibly high. This heat fuels the transformation into a Beedrill. If touched, the shell will feel warm to the touch, and the Pokémon inside may extend its poison barbs slightly to discourage further interference.

Environmental Protection

Kakuna often hang from tree branches using thin silk threads. They are frequently mistaken for parts of the tree, allowing them to remain safe while their body undergoes its final metamorphosis. Despite appearances, they are highly aware of their surroundings.

BATTLE PROTOCOLS

Shed Skin (Ability)

Like Metapod, Kakuna can shed its outer layer of skin to cure itself of status conditions. This is its only way to deal with lingering toxins or burns while it waits out the evolution process.

Tactical Defensive
  • Harden: Its primary method of defense. By stiffening its shell, it can minimize damage from Physical attacks significantly.
  • Iron Defense: An advanced reinforcement technique that maximizes its shell's structural integrity.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Eviolite: Just like its counterpart, this item makes Kakuna surprisingly tanky in the early game.
  • Rocky Helmet: Punishes Beedrill's natural enemies (like Pidgeotto) that attempt to make physical contact.
