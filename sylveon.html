Sylveon is one of the distinct evolutionary forms of Eevee. It is a kind and gentle Pokémon that uses its ribbon-like feelers to soothe fighting and dissipate hostile emotions.
Physical Anatomy
  • Feelers: Highly sensitive ribbons that emit a calming aura.
  • Ocular: Large blue eyes that communicate deep affection.
  • Dermal: Soft white and pink fur often kept meticulously clean.
  • Paws: Small paws often highlighted by blue or pink tips.
Behavorial Pattern
Commonly found near urban areas due to its close connection to trainers. It wraps its feelers around its trainer's arm to show affection and trust. It thrives on deep bonds.
