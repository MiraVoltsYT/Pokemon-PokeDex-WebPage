BIOLOGICAL OVERVIEW

Sensory Ribbon Feelers

Sylveon wraps its ribbon-like feelers around the arms of its beloved trainer to learn their feelings. These feelers emit a soothing aura that can stop a battle instantly by erasing all hostile intent from its opponent.

The Bond Trigger

Unlike other evolutions, Eevee only transforms into Sylveon when it possesses a Fairy-type move and shares a deep, soulful bond with its trainer. It is the physical manifestation of trust and affection.

Hunting Tactics

Despite its gentle appearance, it is a formidable hunter. It distracts prey with its fluttering ribbons, lowering their guard before striking with surprising precision and mystical power.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Pixilate (Hidden Ability)

This elite ability converts all Normal-type moves into Fairy-type moves and boosts their power by 20%. This makes Sylveon's **Hyper Voice** one of the most devastating sounds on the battlefield.

The Special Tank

With a massive Base 130 Special Defense, Sylveon can survive powerful mystical attacks that would knock out other Pokémon. It often uses **Wish** and **Protect** to keep itself and its teammates healthy during long engagements.

Recommended Gear
  • Choice Specs: Maximizes the damage of Pixilate Hyper Voice.
  • Leftovers: Provides steady healing for a more defensive, support-based role.
  • Pixie Plate: A middle-ground boost for players who need to switch between offensive and supportive moves.
