Biological Overview

Galvantula is a predatory arachnid that populates magnetic caves. It is covered in thick, static-charged fur that can discharge high-voltage shocks. Unlike standard spiders, its "silk" is a hyper-conductive fiber that acts as an extension of its nervous system.

Hunting Behavior

It creates massive, nearly invisible webs of electrified silk. When prey becomes entangled, Galvantula doesn't just wrap them; it sends a surge of electricity through the web to paralyze them instantly. It is highly territorial and will defend its nesting grounds with aggressive lunges.

Joltik Symbiosis

Galvantula protects swarms of its pre-evolution, Joltik. While Joltik are often seen clinging to larger Pokémon to drain energy, they return to Galvantula nests to share the harvested electricity, fueling the hive's defensive webs.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Compound Eyes (Ability)

Galvantula possesses multiple optical sensors that calculate wind speed and target movement. This increases accuracy by 30%, allowing it to use high-power moves like **Thunder** with almost perfect reliability.

Signature Tactics
  • Sticky Web: Its most feared move. It coats the opponent's field in silk that lowers the Speed of any Pokémon that switches in.
  • Volt Switch: Galvantula strikes with electricity and immediately retreats back into its Pokéball, maintaining momentum.
Recommended Gear
  • Focus Sash: Ensures Galvantula survives at least one hit to set up its Sticky Web.
  • Life Orb: Boosts its Special Attack damage by 30% for a "Glass Cannon" build.
  • Wide Lens: For players who want 100% guaranteed hits on almost every move in its arsenal.
