ARCHIVE: THE HARDY SCAVENGER

Hardy Vitality

This Pokémon is cautious in the extreme. Even while asleep, it constantly moves its ears to listen for danger. Its impressive vitality allows it to survive in any environment, from fields to city sewers.

Incisor Growth

Its fangs grow continuously throughout its life. To keep them worn down to a manageable size, Rattata must gnaw on hard objects like logs, rocks, or even the walls of houses.

Alolan Adaptation

In Alola, Rattata became nocturnal to avoid Yungoos. This led to a Dark/Normal-type variant with black fur and a highly developed sense of smell for finding fresh food.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Run Away / Guts (Abilities)

Guts is its most dangerous trait; its Attack power increases by 50% if it is suffering from a status condition like Burn or Poison.

Signature Moveset
  • Hyper Fang: A powerful bite with sharp front fangs that may cause the opponent to flinch.
  • Super Fang: A tactical strike that always cuts the target's current HP exactly in half.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Flame Orb: Used to self-inflict a burn, instantly activating the Guts ability for maximum damage.
  • Focus Sash: Ensures Rattata survives at least one hit to land a crucial Endeavor or Super Fang.
