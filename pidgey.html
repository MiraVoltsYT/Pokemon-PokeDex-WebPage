ARCHIVE: THE COMMON SKY SCOUT

Docile Nature

A common sight in forests and woods. It is very docile—it will often kick up sand to protect itself rather than fight back. It is the ideal companion for novice trainers due to its predictable behavior.

Internal Navigation

It has an extremely sharp sense of direction and homing instincts. It can return to its nest from vast distances, even if it has never explored the route before.

Wing Maneuverability

While small, its wing muscles are highly developed. By flapping them rapidly near the ground, it can create a localized dust storm to obscure vision.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Keen Eye / Tangled Feet (Abilities)

Keen Eye prevents its accuracy from being lowered. Tangled Feet raises its evasion if it becomes confused.

Signature Moveset
  • Gust: A basic wind-based attack that strikes from a distance.
  • Sand Attack: Its primary defense mechanism; lowers the opponent's accuracy.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Sharp Beak: Boosts the power of Flying-type moves.
  • Eviolite: Boosts Defense/Sp.Def for non-fully evolved Pokémon.
