BIOLOGICAL DATA: THE LIVING BUNKER

Hardened Carapace

A Metapod's shell is as hard as a sheet of iron. It prepares for evolution by keeping its body inside a tough shell, which protects the soft internal organs as they reorganize into a butterfly structure. While it appears motionless, the inside is in a state of high-speed liquid transition.

Vulnerability During Growth

If the shell is cracked, its insides might leak out. Because the shell is so heavy and rigid, Metapod can only move by wiggling slightly. It relies entirely on its camouflage in the forest to avoid being spotted by predators while it is stationary.

BATTLE PROTOCOLS

Shed Skin (Ability)

Every turn, Metapod has a 33% chance to cure itself of any status conditions (like Sleep, Burn, or Poison). This makes it surprisingly difficult to wear down with passive damage while it sits and hardens its shell.

Signature Moveset
  • Harden: The definitive move. Metapod stiffens every muscle in its body to boost Physical Defense. In the wild, two Metapod may engage in "Harden battles" until both are as solid as stone.
  • Iron Defense: A more advanced version of Harden that raises Defense by two stages instead of one.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Eviolite: Turns Metapod into an immovable object. Boosting its 55 Defense by another 50% makes it incredibly difficult for early-game physical attackers to break.
  • Rocky Helmet: Punishes anyone brave enough to make physical contact with its iron-hard shell.
