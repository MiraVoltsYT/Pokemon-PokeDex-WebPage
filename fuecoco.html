Species Log
Fuecoco lies on warm rocks and uses the heat to create fire energy. It has a calm and steady disposition, but when it sees food, it charges with surprising speed.
Thermal Anatomy
  • External Scales: Square-shaped scales that absorb and store solar heat.
  • Fire Sac: Internal organ constantly overflowing with fire energy.
  • Sprouts: The two yellow scales on its head are fire energy leaking out.
Tactical Note
Due to its Unaware ability, Fuecoco ignores the opponent's stat changes, making it an excellent wall against setup-heavy teams.
