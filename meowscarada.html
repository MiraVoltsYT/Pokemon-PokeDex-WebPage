Biological Overview

Meowscarada uses the reflective lining of its cape to manipulate light, creating illusions that allow it to "disappear" mid-battle. Its fur is impregnated with a sweet-scented pollen that causes opponents to lose focus and become entranced by its movements.

Behavioral Patterns

This Pokémon is notoriously proud and sensitive. If its trainer gives more attention to another Pokémon, Meowscarada may act out or feign indifference. It is a master of sleight-of-hand, often stealing items from unsuspecting foes without them noticing.

BATTLE STRATEGY

Flower Trick (Signature Move)

Meowscarada sets a rigged flower bomb on the target. This move **never misses** and is **guaranteed to result in a Critical Hit**, bypassing all defensive stat boosts the opponent might have.

Protean (Hidden Ability)

A rare trait among the species. Meowscarada changes its type to match the move it is about to use, granting it a permanent 1.5x STAB boost for any attack once per switch-in.

Held Items
  • Focus Sash: Guarantees a move even if hit by a super-effective attack.
  • Choice Band: Maximizes its physical wall-breaking potential.
