ARCHIVE: THE TRIPLE-STINGER PREDATOR

Swarm Intelligence

Beedrill are extremely territorial and never live alone. They fly at high speeds and attack in swarms. If a traveler disturbs their nest, the entire colony will pursue the intruder relentlessly using the three venomous stingers on their forelegs and abdomen.

Pinpoint Lethality

It takes pride in its speed. It flies in, stabs the enemy multiple times with its poisonous stingers, and then flies away before the opponent can react. The poison from the tail stinger is the most potent and is fast-acting.

Mega Evolution: The Apex Bug

Upon Mega Evolving, its wings grow into six high-frequency rotors and its legs become massive lances. Its **Adaptability** ability doubles the power of its Bug and Poison moves, making its **U-turn** attack one of the most dangerous hit-and-run moves in history.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Swarm / Sniper (Abilities)

Beedrill can either power up its Bug moves when near defeat (Swarm) or deal critical hits that do significantly more damage than normal (Sniper). It is built for aggressive, high-risk playstyles.

Signature Moveset
  • Twineedle: A unique move that strikes twice in one turn. Each hit has a chance to poison the opponent.
  • Fell Stinger: If Beedrill knocks out an opponent with this move, its Attack stat is drastically boosted, allowing it to sweep entire teams.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Beedrillite: Necessary for Mega Evolution. Transforms Beedrill's Speed and Attack into legendary tiers.
  • Focus Sash: Because its Defense is so low, this prevents Beedrill from being "one-shot" before it can land a hit.
