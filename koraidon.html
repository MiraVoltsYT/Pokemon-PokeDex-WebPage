Prehistoric Overview

Koraidon is a Paradox Pokémon hailing from a period millions of years in the past. Its biology is purely organic, featuring massive throat sacs and feathers that behave like adaptive solar panels. Its muscular structure is far denser than any modern Dragon-type.

Behavioral Patterns

While Miraidon is analytical, Koraidon is instinctive. It is highly protective of its territory and forms deep emotional bonds. It consumes massive amounts of nutrients to maintain its "Apex Mode," with a recorded obsession for sandwiches containing high-protein fillings.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Orichalcum Pulse (Ability)

When Koraidon enters battle, it releases a primal roar that summons **Harsh Sunlight**. This ancient weather pattern activates its internal metabolism, increasing its Attack stat by 33.3%.

Evolutionary Path

  • Sprint/Swimming Mode: Uses its powerful legs and tail for rapid movement across land and water.
