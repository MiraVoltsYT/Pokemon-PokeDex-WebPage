ARCHIVE: THE FEROCIOUS GNAWER

Whiskers & Balance

It uses its long whiskers to maintain balance. It apparently slows down if they are cut off. Its webbed hind feet allow it to cross rivers and hunt for aquatic prey.

Destructive Chewing

Its gnawing fangs are strong enough to chew through cinder blocks and heavy doors. It is a major pest to human structures, often causing buildings to collapse by weakening supports.

Aggressive Nature

Raticate is highly territorial. When it becomes angry, it stands on its hind legs and squeals loudly to intimidate rivals before lunging with its fangs.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Guts / Hustle (Abilities)

Hustle increases Attack power but lowers accuracy. Guts is the preferred strategy, making Raticate a powerhouse when afflicted by status conditions.

Signature Moveset
  • Facade: Its power doubles if the user is burned, paralyzed, or poisoned. Paired with Guts, this hits like a truck.
  • Sucker Punch: Allows Raticate to strike first if the opponent is preparing an attack, compensating for higher-speed enemies.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Flame Orb: The standard equipment for a Guts Raticate to trigger its power boost immediately.
  • Silk Scarf: Boosts the power of Normal-type moves like Facade and Hyper Fang.
