BIOLOGICAL ANOMALY

The Disguise

A lonely Pokémon, it conceals its terrifying appearance beneath an old rag so it can get closer to people and other Pokémon. Its disguise is modeled after Pikachu, the most popular Pokémon from 20 years ago.

Lethal Curiosity

Legend says that a scholar who once looked underneath Mimikyu's rag died from a mysterious, painful shock. To this day, the true form of Mimikyu remains the greatest mystery in Alolan biology.

Vengeful Spirit

If its neck is broken during a fight, or its rag is torn, it will stay up all night frantically patching it back together. If someone is responsible for the tear, it will hunt them down with relentless spite.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Disguise (Ability)

Mimikyu's signature trait. The cloth acts as a decoy, taking the impact of the first attack aimed at it. This "free turn" allows Mimikyu to set up power-boosting moves like **Swords Dance** safely.

The "Let's Snuggle Forever" Z-Move

Using its Fairy-type energy, Mimikyu traps its opponent inside its rag. What happens inside is unknown, but the psychological and physical damage is catastrophic.

Technical Tools
  • Life Orb: Sacrifices some HP for a massive 30% boost to its Shadow Claw and Play Rough attacks.
  • Mental Herb: Prevents opponents from using "Taunt" to stop Mimikyu's setup moves.
