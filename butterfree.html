ARCHIVE: THE WINGED TOXINOLOGIST

The Toxic Scales

Butterfree’s wings are coated with water-repellent scales. This allows it to fly in the rain without getting weighed down. However, these scales are also highly toxic powders. When it flaps its wings hard, it releases these powders into the air to paralyze or poison attackers.

Compound Vision

Its large, red eyes are composed of thousands of tiny lenses. This allows Butterfree to see in extreme detail, helping it locate even the smallest flowers from a distance or track the movements of swift-moving enemies.

Gigantamax Potential

When Gigantamaxed, its wings glow with a blinding light. Its signature move, **G-Max Befuddle**, scatters scales that cause Poison, Paralysis, or Sleep to every single opponent on the field simultaneously.

BATTLE PROTOCOLS

Compound Eyes (Ability)

This is Butterfree’s secret weapon. It boosts its accuracy by 30%. This makes moves like **Sleep Powder** almost 100% accurate, allowing Butterfree to shut down dangerous threats immediately.

Signature Moveset
  • Quiver Dance: A legendary setup move that raises Sp. Atk, Sp. Def, and Speed all at once.
  • Hurricane: A devastating Flying-type move. Under the accuracy boost of Compound Eyes, it becomes much more reliable.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Focus Sash: Since Butterfree has lower defenses, the Sash ensures it can survive one hit to set up a Quiver Dance or Sleep Powder.
  • Wide Lens: If you really want to guarantee those status moves never miss, even against evasive foes.
