ARCHIVE: THE ANCIENT SKY STALKER

Stamina-Based Flight

With its huge and magnificent wings, it can keep aloft without ever having to land for rest. It uses thermals to glide for days, searching its territory for the slightest movement.

Long-Reach Hunting

Its trademark long neck and beak are highly efficient for catching prey in soil or water. It can strike with the speed of a spear, giving its target zero time to escape.

Territorial Menace

Fearow is notoriously difficult to train because of its wild, aggressive temper. It rarely tolerates others in its airspace and will dive-bomb intruders at high velocities.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Keen Eye / Sniper (Abilities)

Fearow leverages Sniper to turn critical hits into match-ending blows. This pairs perfectly with moves that have a high critical-hit ratio.

Signature Moveset
  • Drill Peck: A spinning, spear-like attack. One of the most reliable Flying-type moves in the Kanto region.
  • Drill Run: A Ground-type move that allows Fearow to counter Electric and Steel types that usually resist its wings.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Scope Lens: Increases the chance of critical hits, maximizing the Sniper ability potential.
  • Choice Band: Sacrifices move flexibility for a 50% boost in Attack, turning Fearow into a true "one-shot" threat.
