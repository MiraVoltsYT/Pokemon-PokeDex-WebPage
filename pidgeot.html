ARCHIVE: THE SKY OVERLORD

Mach-2 Flight

By flapping its wings as hard as it can, Pidgeot can generate a whirlwind powerful enough to bend tall trees. It can fly at an altitude of 3,300 feet and reaches speeds of Mach 2.

Intimidating Plumage

Its beautiful, glossy feathers are often used by trainers to identify its health. It spreads its wings wide to intimidate enemies, appearing much larger than its actual size.

Mega Evolution: Gust Master

When it Mega Evolves, its muscular strength grows significantly. With its **No Guard** ability, its winds become so precise that every attack—even the inaccurate **Hurricane**—is guaranteed to land a direct hit.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Keen Eye / Tangled Feet (Abilities)

Pidgeot relies on its superior vision and speed. In battle, it is often used as a "Pivot," coming in to deal damage and quickly switching out using **U-turn**.

Signature Moveset
  • Hurricane: A devastating Flying-type move that can confuse the target. Becomes 100% accurate in rain.
  • Brave Bird: A high-power physical strike where Pidgeot tucks its wings and dives like a missile.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Pidgeotite: Essential for Mega Evolution. Elevates Special Attack and Speed to elite levels.
  • Choice Specs: Boosts Special Attack power by 50% but locks the user into one move.
