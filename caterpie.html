BIOLOGICAL DATA: THE CANOPY CRAWLER

The Osmeterium Antenna

The bright red antenna on its head is actually an organ called an osmeterium. If Caterpie is frightened or attacked, it releases a horrible, pungent odor from this organ to repel predators like Pidgey.

Adhesive Suction

Its feet are tipped with suction pads that allow it to climb any surface—including vertical walls and ceilings—without effort. This is essential for reaching the tender young leaves it prefers to eat at the top of trees.

Voracious Appetite

Caterpie has a voracious appetite. It can eat leaves larger than its own body in a single sitting. This rapid consumption of nutrients is necessary to fuel its incredibly fast evolution cycle.

TACTICAL ANALYSIS

Shield Dust (Ability)

This unique ability protects Caterpie from the secondary effects of moves. For example, if it is hit by a move that has a chance to burn or flinch, Shield Dust completely negates that side effect.

Signature Moveset
  • String Shot: It ejects silk from its mouth to entangle the opponent, drastically lowering their Speed stat.
  • Bug Bite: A physical attack that also allows Caterpie to steal and eat the opponent's held Berry.
Field Gear Recommendation
  • Eviolite: While Caterpie’s base stats are low, an Eviolite can help it survive long enough to land a crucial String Shot.
  • Focus Sash: Ensures Caterpie can survive at least one hit from a stronger predator to get its silk moves off.
