Biological Overview

Miraidon is classified as a Paradox Pokémon, a mysterious entity recovered from the lowest depths of Area Zero. Unlike biological lifeforms, Miraidon's structure appears to be a fusion of highly advanced futuristic technology and dragon-like organic features. Its "skin" is a hyper-conductive metallic alloy that glows with internal neon energy.

Behavioral Patterns

Miraidon is exceptionally intelligent and analytical. While Koraidon relies on primitive instinct, Miraidon scans its environment with infrared sensors. It is stoic and rarely aggressive unless its "Rider" or territory is threatened. Interestingly, researchers have noted its refined tastes—it is notoriously picky about its food, showing a distinct preference for sandwiches made with rare Herba Mystica over standard Pokémon kibble.

The Future Descendant

Scientific data suggests Miraidon is a descendant of the Pokémon Cyclizar from thousands of years in the future. In this timeline, it has evolved (or been modified) to utilize jet propulsion and pure electrical currents for movement, rendering the organic wheels of its ancestor obsolete.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Hadron Engine (Ability)

Upon entering battle, Miraidon activates its internal reactor. This creates **Electric Terrain**, which supercharges the Pokémon's Special Attack stat by approximately 30%. This makes Miraidon one of the most dangerous offensive threats in existence.

Transformation Modes

Miraidon is capable of shifting its physical form to suit its needs:

  • Ultimate Mode: Its standing combat form, where its energy whiskers fully deploy.
  • Drive/Aquatic/Glide Mode: Its wheels and jet thrusters activate for rapid travel across any terrain.
Competitive Gear
  • Choice Specs: Multiplies Sp. Atk by 1.5x. Combined with Hadron Engine, it can one-shot almost any opponent.
  • Assault Vest: Capitalizes on its 115 Sp. Defense, making it a "Tank" that can't be easily taken down by other Special attackers.
  • Life Orb: A balanced boost for players who need to switch moves frequently.
