ARCHIVE: THE BURNING TEMPER

Aggressive Biological Shift

Upon evolution, Charmeleon undergoes a drastic personality shift. It becomes incredibly barbaric and aggressive. It has been observed seeking out battles purely for the thrill of the hunt, using its sharp claws to slash opponents without mercy.

The Blue Flame Anomaly

In moments of extreme rage or during combat with powerful foes, the flame at the tip of Charmeleon's tail will shift from orange to a brilliant bluish-white. This indicates that its internal temperature has reached critical levels, making its fire-type attacks significantly more dangerous.

Predatory Mechanics

It prefers using its tail to trip or knock down opponents before finishing them with its razor-sharp claws. Unlike Charmander, it is very difficult to train and will often ignore commands if it feels it can finish a battle faster on its own.

COMBAT PROTOCOLS

Behavioral Warning

Trainers are warned that Charmeleon may attack its own master if its combat instincts are high. It requires a firm hand and a display of dominance to remain loyal during this developmental stage.

Tactical Moveset
  • Slash: A high-accuracy physical strike with its developed claws. Higher critical hit ratio.
  • Dragon Rage: A fixed-damage move that ignores defensive stats, often overwhelming mid-tier opponents instantly.
Recommended Gear
  • Eviolite: Still essential. Since it is an unevolved middle stage, this item makes Charmeleon much tankier during the grind to level 36.
  • Scope Lens: Boosts the critical hit ratio even further, pairing perfectly with the move **Slash**.
